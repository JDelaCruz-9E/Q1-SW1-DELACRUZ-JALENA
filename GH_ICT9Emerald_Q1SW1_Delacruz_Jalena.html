Danganronpa is a visual novel murder mystery game created by Spike Chunsoft. The series is mainly about various highschool students who are forced to eliminate each other by a mysterious robotic bear, Monokuma. The only way to escape is to eliminate someone and not get voted out as the "blackened" (also known as the culprit). If they uncover the blackened at the end of the trial, then they will only recieve punishment. However if they vote out the wrong one, everyone but the blackened will be punished.
